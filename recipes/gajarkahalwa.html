Gajar ka Halwa (Carrot delight)


Its Specialty

Gajar ka Halwa is a famous Punjabi dessert that is widely popular in North India, especially during the winter months and Diwali season. This nutritious dessert from India can be enjoyed both hot and cold.

Gajar ka Halwa in English translates to "Carrot Pudding". This sweet carrot pudding also known as Gajrela or Carrot Halwa is super easy to make, but you have to be patient with the cooking process.

Ingredients

  • Ghee: This recipe uses a total of 2 tablespoons of ghee
  • Milk: Recommended to use whole milk for this recipe
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom Powder
  • Cashews

Steps to make the dish

  1. Peel the carrots. Grate the carrots.
  2. Heat a wok on medium heat, once hot, add 1 tablespoon of ghee
  3. Add the grated carrots and saute for about 8 minutes until color of the carrot changes
  4. Keep stirring to avoid burning
  5. Add milk to the grated carrots. Cook the carrots and milk for approximately 6 minutes until all the milk is absorbed by the carrots
  6. Add the sugar
  7. Add 1 more tablespoon of ghee
  8. Keep stirring. until all the water is absorbed which is left behind by the sugar. This process will take approximately 5 minutes.
  9. Once done, add cardamom powder. Stir
  10. Add choped cashews
  11. Mix well
  12. Dry roast the Carrot Pudding for a couple more minutes so the color of the carrots deepens slightly. Turn off the stove
